Overall Look and Feel of KEYS
===============================

The overall look and feel should be modern. We will avoid 3d buttons and 3d metal looks.
The website will have our logo on top of every page(left side). Our nav header will consist of a drop down menu. 

Colours
========
For Keys, colours expose our modern product with very calming light blue to dark blue color scheme.
These are our color swatches:

- #91E5F6
- #84D2F6
- #59A5D8
- #386FA4
- #133C55

Typography
==========
We will be using Poppins from Google Fonts.

Links
=====
Links will not be underlined and should change color when a user hovers over it.

Buttons
=======
our buttons will be flat and squared.
