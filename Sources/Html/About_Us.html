Development

Development
KEYS is constantly trying to improve its products and make the experience as effortless as possible for the customers. We plan to continue to develop faster and more affordable technology with our customer feedback to guide us.

More Models
K.E.Y.S is planning to forge ahead with the creation of multiple other models, to provide variety in the choices we offer. We plan to throw in some fun colours and patterns on the actual designs so that the device appearance can be catered to every individual.

KEYS Overload
Our watches right now allow the user to connect with the storage space through the device itself, but our engineering team is dilegently working on giving you a choice: you can go to KEYS or we can bring KEYS to you. Our next big thing is going to be a chip, that would convert any digital device into a KEYS-approved functional portal into your storage space.

New Feature (Coming Soon): the ability to transfer items in the digital space with contact members who also own a KEYS watch.

Inclusivity

Inclusivity
As our product stands now, we are limiting select types of users from reaping the benefits of this technology. As such, our goal is to make KEYS watches inclusive to everyone. Some areas we paln to address include the following:

Impaired Visibility
Using our watches heavily rely on sight; however, those who have impaired vision would then be missing out on a lot of conveniences of every-day life. Within the next couple of years, we are planning to include a fully functional AI personnel to navigate people through the different functions of KEYS watches. This feature would not only allow for people with disabilities to live a life free of 'hauling' and 'carrying', but also for any individual to ask our digital friend whether they have a certain item in the storage space from a distance!

Language Barriers
Currently, both the website and the product use English to communicate events, intructions, and alerts, but the reality of the matter is that not everyone knows English. By integrating different language options, we wish to relieve this device of language barriers and permit users to interact with the functions in a multitude of languages!

Sustainability

Sustainability
As a technology company that offers devices made of metal to the public, our impact on the environment cannot be overlooked. We don't wish to rip nature of all its glory, so our future goals include the following aspects:

Ethical Sourcing
KEYS watches are made from 70% recycled materials; however, we feel that the 30% extracted from the earth is still quite impactful on mother nature. We want to bring that 70% all the way up. Our aim is to create a KEYS watch that is 100% recyclable and until that day comes, we can assure all users of KEYS that our team only receives their materials from trusted ethical companies, which are known to extract those metals in the safest way possible.

Donating to a Cause
As our world transends deeper into technology, our footprint on the planet often gets ignored or forgotten. K.E.Y.S wants to bring to light the important issues that affect the environment every day, so we make sure to always donate to those environmental causes with every purchase. However, we plan to do more. We want to host events to raise awareness about the state of our environment and call people to action, in order to inspire change.
