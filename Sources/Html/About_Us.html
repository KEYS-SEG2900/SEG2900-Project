Inclusivity

Inclusivity

As our product stands now, we are limiting select types of users from reaping the benefits of this technology. As such, our goal is to make KEYS watches inclusive to everyone. Some areas we paln to address include the following:

Impaired Visibility
Using our watches heavily relies on sight; however, those who have impaired vision would then be missing out on a lot of conveniences of every-day life. Within the next couple of years, we are planning to include a fully functional AI personnel to navigate people through the different functions of KEYS watches. This feature would not only allow for people with disabilities to live a life free of 'hauling' and 'carrying', but also for any individual to ask our digital friend whether they have a certain item in the storage space from a distance!

Language Barriers
Currently, both the website and the product use English to communicate events, intructions, and alerts, but the reality of the matter is that not everyone knows English. By integrating different language options, we wish to relieve this device of language barriers and permit users to interact with the functions in a multitude of languages!
