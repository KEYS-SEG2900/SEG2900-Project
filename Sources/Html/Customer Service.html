Hardware

For any hardware problem:

- Try doing a hard reboot of the device

- Download the latest software update

If both of these solutions do not work, please visit the closest K.E.Y.S. store for IT support.

If your device is broken and still under warranty*, visit your nearest K.E.Y.S. store to get it repaired of replaced free of charge.

* If the warranty has expired, K.E.Y.S. can not replace the device free of charge. The customer will have to pay for the repairs or a new device.
